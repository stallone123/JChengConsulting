On the Radar · 2026

Project X #001 —
River Jordan

High-potential, early-stage exploration project. The Jordan River (King Fissure) deposit, located on the northeastern slopes of Mount Copeland near Revelstoke, British Columbia, contains significant mineral resources across multiple well-defined zones.

The deposit sits within the Shuswap Metamorphic Complex, characterized by stratabound, disseminated, and massive sulphide layers with an exceptional track record of historical sampling results.
